What I am currently reading

Fahrenheit 451 by Ray Bradbury
Imagine a world where reading literature is a crime and owning books can get you executed. Ray Bardbury's book is set in a distopian future North America, where the most illegal commodities are books; considered the source of all discord and unhappiness.

One Piece by Eiichiro Oda
The story is incredible. The author Eiichiro Oda who originally intended to run for only five years, celebrates its 23rd annversary this year in 2020, and is the best selling manga of all time. If you are interested in picking up something new during quarintine, I highly recommened starting One Piece. You won't be disappointed.

Can't Hurt Me by David Goggins
An inspiring story of a man who overcomes a lifetime of struggle and proved that you can achieve anything if you are willing to push yourself. This is not your typical self-help book, the man lived through the struggle and is a living example of everything he talks about in this book.
